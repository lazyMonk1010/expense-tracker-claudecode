Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored encrypted).
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — pages visited, actions taken, and session duration to help us improve the app.

We do not collect payment information, bank account details, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.
  • To send important service-related communications (e.g. password resets).

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Your expense data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services to help operate the platform (e.g. hosting providers, analytics tools). These providers are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with external financial services, banks, or payment processors — all data is entered manually by you.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to access your data, or request its deletion, please contact us via the contact information provided on our website.

We will respond to all privacy-related requests within a reasonable timeframe.
